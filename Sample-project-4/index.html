About Us

Best MultispecialityHospital in Chennai

Meridian Hospital emerges as a prominent corporate healthcare institution, renowned for its global benchmarks in the industry. Our distinction lies in delivering top-notch healthcare services across various specialized tertiary care fields, encompassing the full range of contemporary medical expertise. As a distinguished 300-bed multi-super specialty hospital, we boast modern infrastructure and technology, complemented by a team of top-tier doctors committed to delivering compassionate care. At Meridian, we are driven by a dual focus on accessibility and affordability, ensuring that quality healthcare is within reach for all. Our ethos of professionalism, ethics, and personal responsibility underpins every aspect of our operation, earning the unwavering trust of our patients.


In an era where health challenges loom large, we stand ready with a forward-thinking approach to healthcare. Our LEED Certified green building, equipped for air ambulance services, underscores our commitment to sustainability and innovation. Discover Meridian Hospital, a beacon of excellence in healthcare that sets the standard for superior medical services with a global touch.
