About Us

Exceptional Care Close to you

Meridian Hospital, a name that rises above the rest, for better healthcare services and emerging as best corporate hospital with global standards. Meridian stands for its ability to deliver quality healthcare in all super-speciality tertiary care domains that the medical world could offer.

We are a 300 Bedded Multi-Super Specialty Hospital with state-of-the-art Infrastructure, cutting-edgetechnology and the best-trained doctors to give compassionate care. Today Meridian Hospital is racing towards the goal of providing healthcare to everyone hinging on its two pillars of affordability and accessibility. We pivot our work culture on uncompromised professionalism, ethics and personal responsibility, worthy of the trust our patients place on us.

In a day and age where good health is considered a basic right for every human, and with lifestyle diseases and pandemics challenging the very foundations of healthy living, the necessity for a comprehensive multi-faceted approach to healthcare is paramount.Meridian Hospital is committed to providing advanced healthcare that’s ready for the future. We’ve carefully designed our hospital infrastructure, creating a green building that’s air ambulance-ready and LEED Certified. This makes us a one-of-a-kind healthcare facility in the city.
